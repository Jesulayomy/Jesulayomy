Hi I'm Layomi :wave:, Michael is my middle name. I'm a software engineer bringing over :three: years of full-stack engineering experience, building with ![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/-Python-000?&logo=Python), ![JavaScript](https://img.shields.io/badge/-JavaScript-000?&logo=JavaScript) (and their frameworks), databases like ![MySQL](https://img.shields.io/badge/-MySQL-000?&logo=MySQL) and deploying with ![AWS](https://img.shields.io/badge/-AWS-000?&logo=amazonwebservices) and many other tools.
